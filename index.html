About dot

dot is a lightweight, self-organizing operating system built with a dynamic hardware registry, event-driven architecture, and modular drivers. It scales from microcontrollers to desktop PCs, providing raw access to capabilities like RTC, audio output, PCI/ATA enumeration, and more.

The system emphasizes minimal boot times, clean abstractions, and flexible device integration, making it ideal for experimentation, embedded systems, and rapid prototyping.
